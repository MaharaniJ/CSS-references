1.margin - individual sides

margin-top:value;
margin-bottom:value;
margin-left:value;
margin-right:value;

2.Margin- shorthand properties:

margin:value(apply to all sides)
margin: top right bottom left;
margin:(value(applied to vertically(top,bottom)),value(applied to horizontally(right,bottom)))

same as padding

1.padding - individual sides

padding-top:value;
padding-bottom:value;
padding-left:value;
padding-right:value;

2.padding- shorthand properties:

padding:value(apply to all sides)
padding: top right bottom left;
padding:(value(applied to vertically(top,bottom)),value(applied to horizontally(right,bottom)))
